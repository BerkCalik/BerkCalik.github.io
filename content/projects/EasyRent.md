# EasyRent.com.mt

EasyRent.com.mt is a real estate platform developed for a property agency operating in Malta.  
The website lists rental and sale properties with advanced filtering options, allowing users to quickly find apartments that match their preferences.

Users can submit viewing reservation requests directly through the platform.  
The system is SEO-optimized to ensure high visibility in search engines and improved organic traffic performance.
